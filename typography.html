Typography

Headings

All HTML headings, <h1> through <h6> are available.

Example

H1 - Heading 1

H2 - Heading 2

H3 - Heading 3

H4 - Heading 4

H5 - Heading 5
H6 - Heading 6

Body copy

By default Amazium uses Century Gothic as it's font and a font-size of 15px, with a line-height of 26px. This has been applied to the <body> and to all p (paragraphs). Also the line-height of a p is set to have a margin of 20px by default.

Example

Maecenas faucibus mollis interdum. Donec sed odio dui. Integer posuere erat a ante venenatis dapibus posuere velit aliquet. Sed posuere consectetur est at lobortis.

Intro text

There has been times when we've needed to make a paragraph stand out but for it not to be a headings (h1) so we created the class .intro which makes the font-size & line-height 130% and is perfect introduction text

Example

Sed posuere consectetur est at lobortis. Maecenas sed diam eget risus varius blandit sit amet non magna. Cras justo odio, dapibus ac facilisis in, egestas eget quam.

<p class="intro">...</p>

Emphasis

Make use of HTML's default emphasis tags with lightweight styles.

Example

This paragraph is rendered for fine print.

This paragraph is rendered as bold.

This paragraph is rendered as italicized.

This paragraph contains a abbr word in it.

This paragraph contains a Superscript word in it.

This paragraph contains a Subscript word in it.

This paragraph is has a Highlighted word in it.

<small>This text is rendered for fine print.</small>
<strong>This text is rendered as bold.</strong>
<em>This text is rendered as italicized.</strong>
<abbr title="Abbreviations">abbr</abbr>
<sup>Superscript</sup>
<sub>Subscript</sub>
<span class="highlight">Highlighted</span>

Addresses

Present contact information for the nearest ancestor or the entire body of work.

Example
Your Company Name
123 Something Road,
Your City, AB 12345
T: (123) 456-7890
Your Name
your.name@yourname.com 
<address>
    <strong>Your Company Name</strong><br />
    123 Something Road,<br />
    Your City, AB 12345<br />
    <abbr title="Telephone">T:</abbr> (123) 456-7890
</address>
